Carmel Monastery

Welcome to the Carmel of the Holy Child Jesus Monastery—a sanctuary of silence nestled in the heart of Bethlehem.

Founded by St. Mary of Jesus Crucified, it serves as a home for nuns dedicated to a life of prayer and meditation.

"Mary, this house will be called the house of joy and peace."

— Divine Message

The architecture is deeply symbolic; the monastery is shaped like a star, while the residence reflects the historic Tower of David.
